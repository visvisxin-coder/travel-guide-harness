# Content Invariants

These are non-negotiable rules for travel guide content.

**INV-1.** No invented places, restaurants, ticket rules, prices, opening hours, or transport rules.

**INV-2.** Volatile facts must include a check date or be marked "需出行前确认".

**INV-3.** Every day must have at least morning, afternoon, and evening coverage unless the trip style says otherwise.

**INV-4.** Each day should have no more than 4 major stops.

**INV-5.** Every stop must include place, duration, transport, budget, and reason.

**INV-6.** Every guide must include backup plans for weather and low energy.

**INV-7.** Transport advice must be practical and tied to the route, not generic.

**INV-8.** Food recommendations must include category, budget level, and route fit.

**INV-9.** Final guide must include the last checked date.

**INV-10.** Warnings and tradeoffs must be visible when the route has crowding, booking, weather, or physical-demand risks.

**INV-11.** Every guide must declare `trip_context.visit_type`: `first_time`, `return_visit`, or `local_refresh`.

**INV-12.** If `visit_type` is `return_visit`, the guide must respect `visited_before` and `exclude` lists for attractions, neighborhoods, restaurants, food categories, and experiences.

**INV-13.** Return-visit guides must explain what is new or different from a classic first-time route.

**INV-14.** Transport planning must happen after major stops are selected.

**INV-15.** Every route leg should include a recommended transport mode, approximate time, approximate cost or cost level, alternatives, and pain points.

**INV-16.** If rental car or self-driving is recommended, parking, congestion, city restrictions, and pickup/return friction must be addressed.

**INV-17.** Social trend recommendations must be clearly separated from core itinerary recommendations.

**INV-18.** Xiaohongshu-style trending items must include caveats and a recommended action: `include`, `optional`, `skip`, or `verify`.

**INV-19.** Social trend popularity is not sufficient justification for inclusion in the main route.

**INV-20.** Metro maps must include source/credit metadata, a last checked date, and a disclaimer when not official.

**INV-21.** Metro maps should list route-relevant lines and stations; do not include a map purely as decoration.
