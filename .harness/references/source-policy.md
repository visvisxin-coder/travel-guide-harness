# Source Policy

Travel facts can change quickly. Treat the following as volatile:

- Opening hours
- Ticket prices
- Reservation requirements
- Visa and entry rules
- Transport schedules
- Road closures, congestion rules, rental car restrictions, parking availability
- Restaurant operating status
- Social media trend rankings, influencer claims, new store popularity, exhibition availability

Social platform content is a weak signal. Treat it as inspiration unless verified by official, map, venue, or recent multi-source evidence.
- Temporary closures

Volatile facts must be checked close to the travel date. If they are not checked, write "需出行前确认".
